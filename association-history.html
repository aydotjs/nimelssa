History of The Association

HISTORY OF THE ASSOCIATION

Nigerian Medical Laboratory Science Students Association, University of Ibadan Chapter {NIMELSSA UI}, is an association of Medical Laboratory Science Students in th e University of Ibadan, and it has been in existence for six years in the University.

This association was first presided over by Mr Adewemimo Adefemi, who is now a licensed Scientist. The association has metamorphosed into a family of Medical Laboratory Students over the years in the University.
