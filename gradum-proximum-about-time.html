GRADUM PROXIMUM: IT'S ABOUT TIME!

- Faith Olorunwa

That time of the year is upon us once again in NIMMELSA UI, the time when we all get to briefly abandon the Labs and classrooms, hang the lab coats and gloves, close the pages of our notebooks and textbooks, and launch deep into the world of fun and extra-curricular activities, as we create the needed balance between work and play so that we become sharp jacks. Just so you know, it is the annual Health week, a week like no other, where we have the luxury of a whole week to ourselves. And guess what? It is going to be a lecture free week, when you will be opportuned to unwind and compensate for the rigour and stress of school.

Customarily, the annual Health week is a week-long of activities which include activities like; bonfire, symposium,CHAP, sports day, variety night and the dinner. To whet our appetite for this memorable event, reflections from the previous health weeks would really do. From the curtain raiser, the usually lit bonfire nights, to the the highly intellectual symposiums, the humanitarian CHAP outreaches, the highly competitive sporting activities, variety night which is a form of NIMMELSA's got talent to the talk-of-the-town dinner, I am sure you have not forgotten the "Owanbe" party at SUB event centre last session, we are still trying to wrap our heads around that.

Although not without its own flaws, the 'Next level' team since its inception had always harped on the mantra of doing things differently and innovatively, and this, we are seeing again in the build-up to this year's health week. What we are now witnessing is an injection of fresh ideas into the week like; the magazine launch and the quiz (Triwizard trivia). We all hope that their bark is just as their bite as we keep our fingers crossed.

Last Saturday, the walkathon exercise gave us a foretaste of the enormous fun that awaits us, we hope that that is just a tip of the iceberg and that more aces are up there in the sleeves of the "Next level" team.

When approached as regards this health week, the director of information of NIMMELSA UI, Ajayi Sanmi, hinted that NIMMELSITES have nothing to worry about, as he allayed the accumulated fears that had clouded the certainty of the week holding.

"A lot of works have gone into the preparation for Health Week. Much more work is still going into it at the moment, and will still go into it before the inception of the whole week and during the week. " He said. "This all proves that the health week is going to be one of a kind. So I would say people should expect the best, on another level entirely". He continued.

"Notable persons in the medical world will be gracing our Symposium. We'd be surprised at the calibre of people we see on that day. Transportation will be made available for necessary events. Feeding, Venue and the sorts are of course something Nimelssites should expect more about"

When asked about the calibre of people that should be expected during the week, he mentioned big names like; Incumbent Head of Department, Dr. Mabel Charles-Davies, the pioneer Head of the Department in the person of Professor O.G. Ademowo, the Sponsor of the annual Essay Competition in Nimelssa UI, Dr. V.O. Ogunleye, the Commissioner of Health of Oyo State, Dr Bode Ladipo, Mr Dapo Lam Adesina, among many others.

On sponsorship, he revealed that the NIMMELSA had had varieties of Sponsors and that they are still actively open to more Sponsors from far and wide.

He further called for an all-inclusive participation from all Nimelssites, noting that this is a larger part of what will make the week successful and this actually starts from the dues payment- A major challenge that had faced this administration and had threatened the holding of the health week. He also appealed to all Nimelssites across the levels to physically show up and actively participate in all activities during the week. "With all this in place, it's going to be grandiose", he concluded.

As we earnestly anticipate the arrival of these "grandiose" week, where the kings and queens will slay in grace and style, and the weak look forward to a miracle to bully the strong. Let us keep our preparation in top gear and do all we need to do to make the week an all round success. And more importantly, the comradeship that this week affords should be effectively channeled into calming frayed nerves and healing injured feelings. Shall we...
